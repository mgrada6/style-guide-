Font Family

Open Sans has a dual nature. It has a mechanical skeleton and the forms are largely geometric. At the same time, the font features friendly and open curves.


Roboto should be used to support Open Sans. Roboto reads well at small sizes (mobile), it doesn't take a lot of space and should be used for its utility and legibility. Use Roboto for body copy and smaller text size uses.
