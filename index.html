Font Family

Gotham has a dual nature. It has a mechanical skeleton and the forms are largely geometric. At the same time, the font features friendly and open curves.


Open Sans should be used to support Gotham. Open Sans reads well at small sizes (mobile), it doesn't take a lot of space and should be used for its utility and legibility.
